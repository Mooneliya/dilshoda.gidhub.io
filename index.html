About INHA university in Tashkent

INHA University in Tashkent (IUT) is a branch of INHA University in South Korea. It was established through cooperation between the government of Uzbekistan and the Republic of Korea to train specialists in information technology, computer engineering, and logistics. The university follows the Korean education model, offering internationally recognized programs and a strong focus on technology, innovation, and business.
